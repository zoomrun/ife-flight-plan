Act 4
Now — Week 3. The Same Pattern Is Active Today.
The gate is full again. The same three roles are being asked to service every flight simultaneously. Five Dallas engineers and a full Hyderabad team are at the gate ready to build. They are not building. Not because they are slow. Because the three people who load their planes with context, stories, and direction are being pulled in five directions at once. The engineers are not idle. They are waiting for clarity that nobody upstream has time to give them.
Critical connector 1
Product Owner
Carrying PO responsibilities, PM strategy responsibilities, Hyderabad context transfer, Objective 3 discovery, Objective 2 planning, and inbound work triage simultaneously. No confirmed filter for what can wait.
Structural cause: The PM director role has been functionally vacant for approximately 6 months. The Product Owner is absorbing the gap by design, not by choice. The ownership boundary between two directors has not been formally resolved, which means strategic direction arrives from multiple sources with no single authority to triage it.
Source: PM direct connect, March 16, 2026 • Week 1–2 field observation
Critical connector 2
UX Researcher
Allocated across 8 concurrent IFC initiatives, MVP1 Helsinki, Objective 3 UX design, Objective 2 UX design, Fox Sports, AT&T, and UserZoom research approvals. Named by the PM as one of three people critical to MVP1 delivery.
Structural cause: No director-level prioritization decision has been made on UX research allocation. The system has no mechanism to tell this role which work wins when demands conflict. Every initiative arrives with equal urgency and no named decision owner to sequence them.
Source: PM direct connect, March 16, 2026 • MD Teams channel, March 19, 2026
Critical connector 3
Architect
Carrying MVP1 Helsinki architecture, Objective 3 manifest validation design, AT&T release work, and Objective 2 technical feasibility simultaneously. The team cannot size or sequence work without this role's input.
Structural cause: The architect is being asked to support two delivery tracks and one coaching engagement concurrently with no formal allocation decision. Technical direction and product discovery direction are arriving from different sources with no shared priority order.
Source: PM direct connect, March 16, 2026 • OST session observation, March 16, 2026

Act 5
Three Clearances Needed — Only Leadership Can Issue Them
01
Ownership Clarity
Role: Directors
The IFE product strategy currently has multiple parties providing direction to the same team simultaneously. The ownership boundary between the two directors has not been formally resolved. A confirmed owner removes the competing instructions. The team needs one tower, not three.
If not decided before March 27 The VP Connect produces conversation without accountability. Competing instructions continue. The pattern that produced 69% waste and a 6-month director vacancy persists into the next delivery cycle.
02
Inbound Work Filter
Role: Managing Directors
The team cannot use the product objectives as a filter for inbound work without leadership confirming that non-objective work waits. Two items arrived in two weeks. The pattern is confirmed. At the current rate, 12 more arrive by May 22. Without a filter the three connectors keep absorbing everything.
If not decided before Week 6 The engagement closes with the same structural condition it opened with. The next team inherits the same flight plan. The 16-week delivery improvement the team projected remains unrealized.
03
Research Prioritization
Role: Director of Product
The UX research function is currently allocated across eight concurrent initiatives with no decision on which wins when they conflict. One prioritization decision frees the capacity needed to advance Objectives 2 and 3. Without it neither objective can move at the pace the engagement requires.
If not decided before Week 6 Both objectives stall on UX. The Hyderabad team stays at the gate. Dallas continues without the passenger evidence needed to validate what they are building. The 45% mismatch has no remediation path.
