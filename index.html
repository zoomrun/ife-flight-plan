What needs to change
Three Decisions. Two Fully Open. One Partially Answered. The Team Cannot Close Any Of Them.
01
Ownership Clarity
Role: Director, Aircraft Interiors & IFE Connectivity
The IFE product strategy has multiple parties providing direction to the same team with no confirmed authority to triage what lands first. This produced the 20:1 wait ratio on the Loyalty team dependency in the free WiFi launch. The same condition is active today across the DAL and HYD teams.
Open since March 10 product leadership sync Without a confirmed owner, competing instructions continue. DAL and HYD receive direction from multiple sources with no single authority to sequence it. The 16-week delivery improvement the team projected remains unrealized.
02
Inbound Work Filter
Role: Managing Director, Customer Experience
On March 19 a senior leader stated in the leadership channel: MVP1 always wins when there is a conflict. That is a triage rule, not a structural fix. Two items arrived in two weeks. The rule tells the team what wins when conflicts arise. It does not stop the conflicts from arriving. The three connectors keep absorbing every inbound item. The system producing the conflict remains unchanged.
Partially resolved March 19. Structural condition still open. A triage rule is not a filter. The inbound items keep arriving. DAL and HYD keep waiting. The same mechanism that produced 22 weeks of wait time in the free WiFi launch is still operating. The rule tells the team who wins. The system needs to stop producing the conflict in the first place.
03
Research Prioritization
Role: Managing Director, Travel Rewards Technology
The UX research function is allocated across eight concurrent initiatives with no confirmed sequencing. Objectives 2 and 3 both require substantial UX work to advance. Neither can move at the pace required without a prioritization decision at director level.
Open since March 10 product leadership sync Without this decision, both objectives stall on UX. DAL and HYD remain at the gate. The 45% enrollment mismatch has no remediation path. The same single point of failure that existed during the free WiFi launch persists.
